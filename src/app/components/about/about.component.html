Our Purpose

Our charming cafe, set in the beautiful town of Mount Pleasant, is the perfect place to meet friends and family for a relaxed breakfast, lunch or afternoon cup of coffe and plate of food. Amazing coffee doesn’t just simply happen. It takes a modern approach. It’s the endless pursuit of becoming something better in order to make a lasting impact to elevate those around us.

Our Goal

At the end of the day, Raak can be many things to many people. It can be a very good cup of coffee, or the catalyst for innovative communication. The magic of a coffee shop is that once that environment is created, it becomes not just what the staff makes it, but what the customers make it. A true community happens. Raak does this with three simple staples: great coffee, great food and great performances. We believe there is something for everyone at Raak. Quality for All.
